## Pro Tips for Success

✅ **Personalize the opening:** Use their name and clinic name (shows you researched)

✅ **Lead with pain first:** Start with "I know you spend X hours on WhatsApp" — not the solution

✅ **Make it easy to say yes:** Provide calendar link, not "tell me your availability"

✅ **Emphasize Spanish:** They'll appreciate an AI that actually speaks Spanish well

✅ **Keep it short:** They're busy. Under 200 words is best.

✅ **Be authentic:** This is YOU reaching out, not spam

❌ **Don't:** Use generic language or oversell

❌ **Don't:** Lead with features — lead with their problem

❌ **Don't:** Make it about you — make it about THEIR time saved
